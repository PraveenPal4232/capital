November 2022

We are very excited to open a new channel of regular communication as we bring to you the very first edition of our newsletter. We are now more than 7 years in our journey of supporting the new economy companies in India. We have been fortunate to become lifecycle partners to 150+ exciting startups with capital and financial solutions. We hope this quarterly newsletter gives you a glimpse into all that is happening within this exciting community, and some of the things we think about and spend our time on as we continue to build our labour of love - Trifecta Capital. Since our founding days as a venture debt platform, we’ve come a long way. Today, we manage 3 venture debt funds and a growth equity fund, with amounts invested totalling ~Rs. 4,500Cr. and ~Rs 900Cr. respectively. We also offer a digital platform to startups to manage their treasury through our Financial Solutions vertical.

While seven years is a long time to talk about, we would like to share an update on our activities in this fiscal year till date. Our venture debt funds invested into 35 new-economy ventures since March 2022, across sectors and stages. We are seeing several use cases of venture debt including working capital, capex, inorganic growth or specific projects. The demand and quality of opportunities for venture debt continues to be robust. We are excited to continue investing at levels higher than the past years, and double down our support for the startup ecosystem as it weathers through these uncertain times.

We launched a Rs. 1,875Cr. growth equity fund last year called Trifecta Leaders Fund-I. The equity fund has been set up to continue Trifecta Capital’s role as lifecycle financiers to the startup ecosystem. This fund invests in category-leading technology companies in India. With our portfolio companies in the equity fund, we engage across multiple dimensions including support in capital formation initiatives, IPO preparedness, governance, strategic partnerships, and enabling linkages with India's old economy companies and our LP base.

Through our Financial Solutions offering, which was launched in 2020, we are providing to startups an integrated platform with digital treasury management, expert financial advice and a powerful network of global financial institutions. We have deployed over Rs. 7,000Cr of capital through the platform till date.

Trifecta Capital Portfolio Updates

Venture Debt Fund

In this fiscal year till date, we have provided venture debt to 35 new-economy businesses, across a variety of consumer brands, B2B enterprise services (including SaaS, IoT, commerce, payments and logistics), fintech and agritech startups.

We are very excited about EVs and clean energy, a theme that we believe is at an inflection point of investor interest. While we are still participating selectively and deepening our thesis on this, expect to see a lot more activity from us here!

Growth Equity Fund

Since the first close in July’21, the growth equity fund has invested in 10 marquee tech companies.

Most recently, we invested $15mm in Arzooo, a retail tech platform focused on the consumer electronics and durables industry disrupting the traditional distribution ecosystem. The Arzooo platform allows small retailers to compete with online channels as well as gives them the tools to deliver a best-in-class buying and owning experience for customers. The company already serves 10,000+ retailers. Our co-investors are Japan’s SBI Holdings and existing investors, including Celesta Capital and 3 Lines VC.

We continue to back attractive growth-stage opportunities in the Indian startup ecosystem with this fund.

Financial Solutions Updates

Our Financial Solutions platform solves financial engineering gaps that CFOs face in managing stock and flows of money. With our unique Treasury Management framework, we provide new-age companies a tech platform to manage their treasury in a transparent manner with detailed analytics and insights.

The platform offers market leading tech and research, supported by our deep knowledge of cash flow requirements in venture-backed companies, enabling optimal portfolio construction and risk management. We align with all the stakeholders incl. Founders, CFOs and Investors in offering these capital allocation and management solutions.

Over the last 2 years, the platform has managed treasury for 100+ companies with Rs 7,000Cr. flows. 20+ unicorns are using the platform and several other start-ups being actively advised to manage capital in an efficient manner. Many VC firms also use the platform for their own capital management.
