Dear Reader,

We are very excited to open a new channel of dialogue with you as we bring you the very first edition of our newsletter. We are now more than 7 years in our journey of supporting the new, emerging Indian economy. We have been fortunate to become lifecycle partners to 150+ exciting startups with capital and advisory solutions. We hope that this newsletter gives you a glimpse into all that is happening within this exciting community, and some of the things we think about and spend our time on as we continue to build our labour of love - Trifecta Capital.

While 7 years may be too long to talk about, we would like to share what we have done in this fiscal year till date. Our venture debt funds made investments into 35 new-economy ventures since March 2022 across sectors and stages. With this we have invested over Rs. 4,500Cr in Venture Debt across our funds. We are seeing many more use cases of venture debt, with companies using our capital for not only shoring up reserves, but also for working capital, capex, inorganic growth or specific projects. The demand and quality of opportunities for venture debt continues to be robust. We are excited to continue investing at levels higher than past years, and double down our support for the startup ecosystem as it weathers through these uncertain times.

Our equity fund, Trifecta Leaders Fund-I, launched last year, is a Rs. 1,875Cr growth/late stage fund investing in category-leading technology companies in India. Since the first close in July’21, the fund has invested in 10 marquee companies including Dailyhunt, Meesho, Livspace, Country Delight, The Good Glamm Group, PharmEasy, ixigo, Pristyn Care, Cars24 and Arzooo. We continue to back attractive growth/late-stage opportunities in the Indian startup ecosystem with our equity fund.

Through our financial solutions, we are providing digital treasury management solutions to startups, expert advice and a powerful network of global financial institutions. The flagship treasury platform has received more than Rs. 7,000Cr of capital float under management.

Venture Debt Fund Updates

Our venture debt funds made investments into 35 new-economy businesses since March 2022, across a variety of exciting consumer brands, B2B enterprise services (including SaaS, IoT, commerce, payments and logistics), fintech and agritech startups. We are particularly excited about EVs and clean energy, a theme that we believe is at an inflection point of investor interest. While we are still participating selectively and deepening our thesis on this, expect to see a lot more activity from us here!

Our recent investments are skewed in volume to [19 deals out of 36 proportion of deals] early stage (Series A/B) businesses, and in value to [300Cr out of 759Cr - proportion of deal value] growth and late stage businesses.

Equity Fund Updates

Through our equity fund to support growth/late-stage startups in their financing journey, we are engaging with startups across multiple vectors including preparing portfolio companies for an IPO, governance and board representation, creative financing solutions for future rounds and building a bridge between India's old and new economies by fostering connectivity and business collaboration between our LP base and our founders.

Most recently, the equity fund invested $15mm in Arzooo, a retail tech platform focused on the consumer electronics and durables industry disrupting the traditional distribution ecosystem. The platform allows small retailers to compete with online channels as well as gives them the tools to deliver a best-in-class buying and owning experience for customers. The company already serves 10,000+ retailers. Our co-investors are Japan’s SBI Holdings and existing investors, including Celesta Capital and 3 Lines VC.

Financial Advisory Updates

Our Financial Solutions (FinSol) platform, which was launched in 2020, solves financial engineering gaps that CFOs face in managing stock and flows of money. With our unique Treasury Management framework, we provide new-age companies a tech platform to manage their treasury in a transparent manner with detailed analytics and insights. The platform offers market leading tech and research, supported by our deep knowledge of cash flow requirements in venture-backed companies, enabling optimal portfolio construction and risk management. We align with all the stakeholders incl. Founders, CFOs and Investors in offering these capital allocation and management solutions. Over the last 2 years, the platform has managed treasury for 100+ companies with Rs 7,000Cr. flows. 20+ unicorns are using the platform and several other start-ups being actively advised to manage capital in an efficient manner. Many VC firms also use the platform for their own capital management.
